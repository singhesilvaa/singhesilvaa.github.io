About Me

I’m Singhe Silva, a computer science graduate and researcher with strong interests in machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and data mining. I graduated with First Class Honours from the University of Kelaniya and was awarded the Gold Medal for the Most Outstanding Student of the Faculty of Computing and Technology.

My undergraduate research on predicting social media virality, combining sentiment analysis, image processing, and deep learning, was published at an international conference and received the Best Paper Award. I enjoy building data-driven systems that bridge theoretical foundations with real-world impact.

Currently, I work as a Quality Engineer / Developer at Sysco LABS Sri Lanka, where I develop scalable data testing frameworks and explore AI-driven testing methodologies. I also have teaching experience as a visiting lecturer and tutor, and I aspire to pursue a research-focused career in intelligent systems.
